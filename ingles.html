What is BPO?

Business Process Operation (BPO) is a service model that is aimed at outsourcing high-volume sections of operations with an intermediate to low level of complexity.

At MyACo we implement complete operation flows with an in-house methodology that facilitates adoption in an agile and fast manner. Allowing short implementation start-up times.

MYACO OFFER

Data Entry

Data migration is one of the main activities that can cause extended times for companies to launch products or seize obsolete systems, potentially affecting time to market.

 

The Data Entry service developed at MyACo allows our expert consultants in data management and administration to generate the most appropriate strategy to mitigate extended information migration times between databases / systems that do not always have ease of communication with the levels of security requirements according to the level of data sensitivity.

This service can be presented in two modalities:

Initial data migration and implementation of automatic data transfer processes.

Incorporation of work teams for manual migration under high quality standards in work cells.
